Brand   Brief

In the heart of Alwar, Rajasthan, where royal heritage meets rich history, Chancellor was born. Chancellor stands tall in the FMCG industry, claiming its place with pride and dignity. Alwar’s majestic forts and grand havelis infuse Chancellor with a sense of tradition and excellence. Every drop of our oil carries this legacy, enriching meals with a touch of heritage. Chancellor edible oil is a trusted ally for housewives who cook with passion and care. They choose our oil for its quality and the promise of health and taste.

For every housewife, there is a Chancellor at home―the one who leads and provides. Our oil ensures that the meals cooked are worthy of this head of the house and his family.

Chancellor brings the nostalgic charm of Rajasthan into modern homes, offering quality and best-inclass experience. It is a symbol of royalty, creating memorable meals and moments for families, ensuring that every dish is fit for a Chancellor and his family.
